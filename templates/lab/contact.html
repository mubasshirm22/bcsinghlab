Joining the Lab

Graduate Students

Prospective Ph.D. students should apply through the CUNY Graduate Center or Brooklyn College graduate programs in Biology or Computational Sciences. Mention your interest in the Singh Lab in your application. Feel free to email Prof. Singh with your CV and a brief statement of research interests.

Undergraduate Researchers

Brooklyn College undergraduate students interested in computational biology research are encouraged to contact Prof. Singh directly. Prior experience in programming (Python) or biology is helpful but not required — enthusiasm and commitment matter most.

Postdoctoral Researchers

Postdoctoral positions are filled as funding allows. If you are interested, send a CV, a brief research statement, and contact information for three references to Prof. Singh.
